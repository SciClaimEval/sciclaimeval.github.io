As mentioned in our paper, all samples in the dataset were created by humans. However, human annotators can occasionally make mistakes due to workload, oversight, or other factors. To date, we are aware of only a few problematic samples in the dataset (approximately two cases so far). Nevertheless, to ensure a fair comparison among all participating teams, we have decided to keep the dataset unchanged at this time.

The best run on the primary metric (task 1: pair accuracy, task 2: accuracy) decides your team rank versus other team's best submission. However, we will still show and analyse other submissions than the best one if they differ sufficiently (different approach rather than just hyperparameter tuning). Hence, we encourage all teams to explain each submission and explore different ideas so that we can summarize all approaches instead of only the best ones.
